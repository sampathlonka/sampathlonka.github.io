UCSH-201: Foundations in Calculus

In this subject, the following topics covered

  • Differential Calculus
    • Leibnitz Theorem, Taylor's Theorem
    • Maxima and Minima of one variables
    • Total Differentiation, Errors and Approximations
    • Taylor's series in two variables
    • Maxima and Minima of two or more variables
  • Integral Calculus
    • Definite Integrals and its applications
    • Multiple Integrals
    • Applications in areas, volume and surfaces
  • Vector Calculus
    • Differentiation of vectors, Curves in space
    • Operators: del, Gradient, Divergence and Curl
    • Integration of vectors: Line and Surface Integrals
    • Green's, Stokes and Divergence Theorems
